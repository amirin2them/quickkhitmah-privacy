2. Children and Families Policy commitment

This app’s target audience includes children and families.
We commit to follow Google Play’s Families Policy requirements and all applicable laws and regulations related to children’s privacy (such as COPPA, GDPR where applicable, and other local laws).

In particular:

The app content is appropriate for children and does not contain violent, sexual, or otherwise age‑inappropriate material.

The app does not include any behavior intended to mislead children into making purchases or sharing personal data.

We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics that profile children or track them across apps or websites.

3. Data we collect and how we use it

This app is designed as an offline Quran listening and bookmarking helper.

The app does not require an account or login.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other direct identifiers from users, including children.

The app stores bookmarks, playlists, and app settings locally on the device so users can resume playback and manage listening.

The app does not send audio files, bookmarks, or playlists to any external server.

We currently do not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or other third‑party tracking tools.
If we add such services in the future, we will update this policy and ensure they comply with Google Play Families requirements and children’s privacy laws.

4. Permissions and device access

The app requests the following Android permissions solely to provide its core features:

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / access to media

Purpose: To allow users to browse and select audio files or folders from device storage and create playlists for playback.

The app reads file paths and basic metadata needed for playback only.

The app does not upload or share audio file contents or media library information with external services.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (on older Android versions)

Purpose: To save or update local data related to audio usage (such as media indexes or caches) when required by the system.

The app does not write unnecessary data and does not write data for third‑party use.

SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW (draw over other apps / floating widget)

Purpose: To display a floating playback widget on top of other apps so users can quickly pause, resume, or change tracks.

The widget only shows playback controls and current surah information; it does not capture or record screen content, keystrokes, or other app data.

We do not use these permissions to collect personal or sensitive information from children or adults beyond what is necessary for the described functionality.

5. How we share data

We do not sell user data.

We do not share user data with third parties for advertising or profiling.

Data such as bookmarks, playlists, and settings remains on the device unless the user explicitly backs it up using Android or third‑party tools.

6. Data retention and deletion

Bookmarks, playlists, and settings are stored locally and remain until the user deletes them or uninstalls the app.

Uninstalling the app removes the app and its local data from the device.

If we add cloud backup or account features in the future, we will explain where data is stored, how long it is kept, and how users (or parents) can request deletion.

7. Children’s privacy

Because the app includes children in its target audience, we take additional care:

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We do not use device identifiers or advertising IDs for tracking or profiling children.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information can contact us at the email above so we can remove such data if it ever exists.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
