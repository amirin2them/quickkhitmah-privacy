Privacy Policy for “ختمة الطريق | Quick Khitmah”

Last updated: 24 April 2026

1. Developer information
This app “ختمة الطريق | Quick Khitmah” is developed and published by an individual developer.
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at:
Email: en@salch55.uu.me

2. Data we collect and how we use it

This app is designed as an offline Quran listening and bookmarking helper.

The app does not require you to create an account.

The app does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other direct identifiers.

The app stores bookmarks, playlists, and app settings locally on your device so that you can resume playback and manage your listening.

The app does not send your audio files, bookmarks, or playlists to any external server.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or other third‑party tracking tools (if you add any in the future, you must update this section and clearly describe them).

3. Permissions and device access

The app requests the following Android permissions solely for its core functionality:

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / access to media

Purpose: To allow you to browse and select audio files or folders from your device storage and create playlists for playback.

The app reads file paths and metadata needed for playback.

The app does not upload or share the contents of these files off your device.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (on older Android versions)

Purpose: To allow the app to store or update local data related to your audio usage (for example, media indexes or caches) where required by the system.

The app does not write arbitrary data outside what is necessary for its operation and does not write data to share with third parties.

SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW (draw over other apps / floating widget)

Purpose: To display a floating playback widget on top of other apps so you can quickly pause, resume, or navigate tracks while using your device.

The floating widget only shows controls and current surah information; it does not capture or record your screen content or keystrokes.

We do not use these permissions to collect personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the features described above.

4. How we share data

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or analytics.

Any data (such as bookmarks and playlists) stays on your device unless you manually back it up using Android or third‑party tools.

5. Data retention and deletion

Bookmarks, playlists, and settings are stored locally on your device and remain there until you delete them or uninstall the app.

Uninstalling the app will remove the app and its local data from your device.

If, in the future, we add online backup or account features, this policy will be updated to describe where that data is stored and how it can be deleted.

6. Children’s privacy

This app is not specifically directed to children under 13 and is intended for a general audience.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you choose a target audience that includes children in Google Play, you must also comply with Google Play Families Policies and applicable child‑protection laws; if that changes, this section will be updated accordingly.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
